# Sales Insights


## Problem Statement

AtliQ hardware is a company which delivers computer hardware & peripheral Manufacturers to his clients, which has several branches throughout India. The sales director of the company is facing a lot of issues in terms of understanding how the business is performing and what are all the problem company is facing currently as the sales are not as expected and declining gradually. And whenever he calls the regional managers to get the current status of the sales and market, as a human behaviour, these people Humans are not comfortable in consuming numbers from excel files, which is obvious reason for the frustration.

## Solution
Sales director of the AltiQ hardware, decided to build a PowerBI Dashboard for converting the data into visual representation to make data driven decisions. So, he hired a team of data people to complete this task.

# Insights

A single-page report was created in Power BI Desktop and then published to Power BI Service. The following insights were drawn from the dashboard:

### [1] Total Revenue and Profit Overview:
- Over the span of 4 years, the company generated total revenue of ₹985M.
- The total profit margin during this period was ₹24.7M, with a profit margin percentage of 2.5%.
- The total sales quantity reached ₹2M units.
- In 2020 alone, the company generated revenue of ₹142M by selling 350K units and earned a profit of ₹2.1M.

### [2] Regional Market Performance:

- Delhi NCR is the largest market in terms of revenue, contributing ₹520M, which is 52.8% of the total revenue over 4 years. However, Delhi NCR has a relatively low profit margin of 2.3%.
- Bhubaneshwar stands out in 2020 with the highest profit margin of 10.48%.
- Mumbai leads in profit contribution percentage, accounting for 23.89% of the total profit.

### [3] Low-Performing Markets:

- Bengaluru has the lowest profit margin over the 4 years, with a negative margin of -20.8%.
- In terms of profit contribution percentage by market, Bengaluru also ranks the lowest, with a -0.3% contribution to the total profit.

### [4] Top Customers:
- Electricalsara Stores is the company’s biggest customer, generating a total of ₹413M in revenue over 4 years.

### [5] Top Products:
- The product Prod318 is the highest revenue-generating product, bringing in ₹69M over 4 years.

### [6] Product Type Distribution: 
- The distribution product type generated total revenue of ₹494M.
- The own brand product type also generated revenue of ₹494M over the entire 4 years.


### [7] Revenue and Profit Trends:
- The revenue trend indicates a significant decline in June 2020 compared to the previous year.
- The profit margin was at its lowest in April 2020, marking a critical point for the company’s profitability.

### [8] Revenue Growth Potential:
By focusing on underperforming regions and product categories, AtliQ can potentially increase revenue by at least 7% in the next quarter.
